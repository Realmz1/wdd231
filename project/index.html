Welcome to Desert Drive

Desert Drive is your hub for local car clubs, events, meetups, and spotlights on featured classic vehicles. Our community brings together enthusiasts from across New Mexico, creating a shared space for all things classic cars.

From vintage American muscle to European classics, we celebrate the unique blend of Southwestern culture and classic car cruising through the deserts of New Mexico.
