Site Plan: Desert Drive

A Classic Car Cruises Community of New Mexico

Site Name

Desert Drive - This name was selected because it represents the unique blend of Southwestern culture and classic car cruising through the deserts of New Mexico. It's memorable, location-based, and enthusiast-driven.

Optional domain availability: desertdrivecruises.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of Desert Drive is to provide a hub for local car clubs, events, meetups, and spotlights on featured classic vehicles. The site allows members to register and get involved in car cruises throughout the New Mexico region.

Scenarios

  • When is the next classic car cruise event in my area?
  • Where can I sign up to feature my car or club on the homepage?

Color Schema

  • #2C2C2C (Dark Gray) – Used for headers and background.
  • #EEDC82 (Desert Gold) – Used for buttons and accents.
  • #F5F5F5 (Light Gray) – Used for body background.
  • #D14D72 (Desert Rose) – Hover & call-to-action elements.

Typography

  • Orbitron (headings) – Retro, tech-inspired style to match the vintage car vibe.
  • Roboto (body) – Clean and modern, excellent for readability.

Both fonts will be loaded via Google Fonts and used consistently across all pages.
